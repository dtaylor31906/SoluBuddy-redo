Creating a Single Dilution

  1. Enter the name for the solvent you will use in the dilution
    e.g water

  2. Enter the name or formula for the solute in the stock solution
    e.g HCl

  3. Enter the desired volume of the final solution

  4. Enter the desired final concentration in mol/L

  5. Enter the concentration of the solute in the stock solution in mol/L

  6. Enter your calculation result for the volume of the solute to add
