Creating A Solution by Volumetric Transfer

  1. Enter the name for the solvent you will use in the solution
    e.g water

  2. Enter the formula for the solute you will use in the solution
    e.g NaCl

  3. Enter the molecular weight of the solute in g/mol

  4. Enter the density of the solute in g/mL

  5. Enter the desired total volume of the solution

  6. Enter the desired concentration of the solution

  7. Enter the your guess for the volume of solute to add to achieve your desired concentration
