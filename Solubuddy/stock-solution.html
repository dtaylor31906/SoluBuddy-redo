Creating Solution Using Concentrated Stock by Volumetric Transfer

  1. Enter the name for the solvent you will use in the solution
    e.g water

  2. Enter the formula for the solute you will use in the solution
    e.g CH3OH

  3. Enter the molecular weight of the solute in g/mol

  4. Enter the desired total volume

  5. Enter the desired solution concentration in mol/L

  6. Enter the mass percent of the solute found in the stock (number only)

  7. Enter the density of the solute

  8. Enter your calculation result for the volume of solute you will add
