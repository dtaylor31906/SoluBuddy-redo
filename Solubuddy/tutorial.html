Welcome to Solubuddy!

This website has been designed to help you in creating solutions, dilutions, and calibration standards. To use this site you will select which type of activity you wish to perform, then use the buttons at the top of the corresponding page to select the details of the activity. Instructions for perfoming the activity will appear on the right hand side of the screen to help guide you through the process. After submitting your entries and calculation results a final message will alert you to the accuracy of your calculations. Be aware that the significant figure of our calculations are based on that of the values you enter into an activity. Submitting an answer with the wrong number of significant figures can result in your answer being marked as wrong.

The menu items at the top of the page will help you to navigate to different areas of the site such as educational videos and calculations you have saved
